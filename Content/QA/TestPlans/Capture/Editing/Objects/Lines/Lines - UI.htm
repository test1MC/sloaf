Local Toolbar

General
  • Clicking the line toolbar item on the local frame toolbar will let you draw a line object on a frame.
    • After clicking the object, click to add a new segment of the line. Each additional click will add a new segment, connected by a resize point.
  • Clicking and dragging the line will drag the line object to a new location.
  • No typing on lines.
  • Renders when drawn in various directions.
  • Lines are maintained when re-opening file.
  • Styles can be applied.
  • Holding Shift draws straight lines or rather perfect angles based on 15 degrees
    • Works when initially drawing the line
    • Does not works when modifying existing line
  • Cannot rotate
