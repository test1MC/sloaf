Arrow Tab

UI controls
  • Head Section
    • Length: Effects the length of the arrow head.
    • Width: Effects the width of the arrow head
    • Curve Factor: Effects the curve of the arrow head lines.
    • Curved Head Sides: Toggles on/off the Curve Factor of the head
  • Head Tail Section
    • Tail Length: Effects the length of the head tail (from point to point)
    • Base Width: Effects how wide the Head is, compared to the arrow body
    • Tail Curve Factor: Controls the amount of curve on the arrow tail
    • Curved Head Tail: Toggles on/off the curve head tail factor
