Star Tab

UI Controls:
  • Tip Count: To adjust the number of arms.
  • Dip Length: The higher the number, the deeper the arms.
  • Curved Sides: Curve the sides for a more rounded shape.
  • Curve Factor: The higher the number, the greater the curve.
  • Preview: Will display a preview of the object.
