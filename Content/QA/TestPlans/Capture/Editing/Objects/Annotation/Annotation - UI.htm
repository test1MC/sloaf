Basic Appearance

 

General
  • Clicking and dragging the annotation will drag the whole object, including the pointer
  • Clicking and dragging the icon on the annotation (hover over the annotation to make this appear) will drag just the annotation. The pointer will remain at its current location, while the pointer tail section will get longer or shorter depending on where the annotation is moved to.
  • Pressing F2 will bring up a rich text window
  • Typing with the object selected will bring up a rich text window
  • Holding Shift while manually rotating existing objects (hold ctrl) or groups rotates them at perfect angles based on 15 degrees
    • Does not works initially when drawing (feature request in for that)
