Description:

EPUB (electronic publication) file. EPUB is the most commonly used e-book format and is supported by most modern e-book readers. E-books are quickly becoming a strong competitor to traditional paper books because of their cheaper cost, ability to be digitally downloaded, eco-friendliness, and availability of readers (Kindle, Nook, iPad). Flare can tap into this emerging market quite easily since its project structure is already similar to EPUB’s structure.

 

Flare target files will have a new “EPUB” output type that produces a single, self-contained .epub file, which is a specialized ZIP package. The build process will be similar to existing processes such as PDF output. EPUB-specific target options will be added if necessary, and non-applicable target options will be removed from the UI.
