Salesforce

Bug Base Product Area:

  • Integration: Salesforce

 

Description:

Flare is able to publish output files directly to Salesforce. Salesforce does not support JavaScript or external stylesheets. Therefore, only the Clean XHTML format is fully supported. Also, it is recommended that you enable the option Convert stylesheet styles to inline styles, which can be found on the General tab of the Target Editor. This will ensure that your styling is maintained when you publish the Clean XHTML output to Salesforce
